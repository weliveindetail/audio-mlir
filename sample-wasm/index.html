LMS Adaptive Noise Canceller

A swept-low-pass sawtooth — MIDI-triggered from the on-screen piano — buried in colored noise; a 32-tap LMS adaptive FIR learns the noise path and subtracts it. @noise_kind picks the color, @wet sweeps how much of the estimate to remove (0 = noisy, 1 = tone revealed). Each voice runs its own trigger-anchored cutoff LFO through the shared @voice_cut_shape table you draw below; Sweep sets @cut_lfo_step, the LFO speed latched per voice at note-on (so it only affects notes played afterwards). Play a note to hear the canceller pull the tone out of the noise (a rest cancels pure noise — the trivial case).
