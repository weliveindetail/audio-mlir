LMS Adaptive Noise Canceller

A swept-low-pass 440 Hz sawtooth buried in colored noise; a 32-tap LMS adaptive FIR learns the noise path and subtracts it. @noise_kind picks the color, @wet sweeps how much of the estimate to remove (0 = noisy, 1 = tone revealed), and the host streams the cutoff-sweep LFO shape as per-block breakpoints (@lfo_mode = 1); Sweep stretches/compresses that shape.
